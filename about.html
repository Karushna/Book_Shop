About ABC Bookstore

ABC Bookstore is a renowned bookstore located in the heart of Kandy, Sri Lanka. With a rich history spanning over three decades, we have established ourselves as a leading destination for book lovers in the region. Our mission is to provide a diverse selection of books that caters to the varied interests and preferences of our customers.

Our Mission

At ABC Bookstore, our mission is to provide a wide range of high-quality books and literary resources to our customers. We strive to promote a love for reading, encourage lifelong learning, and foster a vibrant literary community. We are committed to offering exceptional customer service and creating a welcoming environment for book enthusiasts of all ages.

Our Vision

Our vision at ABC Bookstore is to be the leading destination for book lovers in Kandy and beyond. We aim to inspire and engage our customers by offering a diverse selection of books across various genres and subjects. We aspire to be a hub for literary events, author talks, book signings, and other cultural activities that celebrate the joy of reading and enhance the intellectual and cultural life of our community.

History

ABC Bookstore has a rich history that spans over three decades. Founded in 1990 by Mr. John Smith, a passionate bibliophile and entrepreneur, the bookstore started as a small shop with a modest collection of books. With a deep commitment to promoting literacy and a strong belief in the power of books to transform lives, Mr. Smith nurtured the store's growth and established it as a trusted and beloved literary institution in Kandy.
